What is this all about?

CODA is a portable, modular,  data acquisition system  designed at the "Thomas  Jefferson National  Accelerator Facility"  (formerly known as CEBAF). Originally CODA was anacronym, CEBAF Online Data Acquisition, but with the renaming of the laboratory in 1996 it was decided that CODA stands for CODA and nothing else.

CODA is a portable,
modular, and above all
configurable data
acquisition system
designed at the "Thomas
Jefferson National
Accelerator Facility"
(formerly known as
CEBAF).
Originally CODA was an
acronym, CEBAF Online
Data Acquisition, but
with the renaming of the
laboratory it has been
decided that CODA
stands for CODA and
nothing else.
